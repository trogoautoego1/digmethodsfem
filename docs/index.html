Welcome


Welcome to our project, Feminist Media Throughout History.

Our goal through this research is to understand how depictions of women and feminism in popular or prominent feminist texts developed over the course of the 19th to 21st century.

The Four Waves of Western Feminism

First Wave- c. 1830s-1920s

Second Wave- c. 1960s-1980s

Third Wave- c. 1990s-2000s

Fourth Wave- c. 2010s-now
