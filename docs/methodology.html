Methodology

Our project aims to compare the Four Waves of Western Feminism through their media, to gain insight into who is considered a part of each movement, what issues are considered a focus, and how each author defines their identity.

We compared each text's mentions of power systems such as patriarchy; religion, whether personal or as a critique; socioeconomic class; author or subject identity such as nationality and sexuality; feminist theories such as political lesbianism or marxist feminism; and social theories such as Fruedian analysis or the Enlightenment individualism.

These are our primary loci of comparison.
